About Us

Built on Vision,
Driven by Excellence

Founded by eminent educationist-philanthropist Dr. Nissar Ahmed, Presidency University was established under the Presidency University Act (Karnataka State Act No. 41) in 2013, as part of the Presidency Group of Institutions — which traces its roots to 1976.

Situated on the Yelahanka-Dodballapur Highway, the 100-acre green campus is just 25 km from Kempegowda International Airport, with a 650-seat auditorium, advanced labs, and 60+ fleet buses.
